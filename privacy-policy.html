Privacy Policy for vibeIn

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to vibeIn. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts (Google, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) to vibeIn, we collect:

  • Basic profile information (name, username, email)
  • Follower counts and engagement metrics
  • Public profile information from connected platforms

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Display your influencer profile within the app
  • Connect you with business opportunities
  • Calculate your reach and engagement metrics
  • Improve our services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your profile data with businesses you choose to connect with through the app.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure authentication protocols.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account
  • Disconnect social media accounts
  • Request data corrections

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate with third-party services (Google, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) subject to their own privacy policies.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@vibein.app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
